Science Notes

1) Biology

  • Cells are the basic unit of life.
  • Photosynthesis: plants convert CO₂ and H₂O into glucose and O₂ using sunlight.
  • Human systems: circulatory transports nutrients and oxygen; respiratory exchanges gases.

2) Physics

  • Newton's First Law: objects maintain motion unless acted upon.
  • Speed = distance/time.
  • Energy conservation: energy cannot be created or destroyed.

3) Chemistry

  • Atoms consist of protons, neutrons, and electrons.
  • Periodic table groups elements by properties.
  • Chemical reactions rearrange atoms into new substances.
