Mathematics Notes

1) Number Concepts

  • Fractions: a/b with b ≠ 0. Simplify by dividing by the GCD.
  • Percent: percent = fraction × 100%.
  • Order of operations: PEMDAS (Parentheses, Exponents, Multiplication/Division, Addition/Subtraction).

2) Algebra Basics

  • Linear equation: ax + b = c → x = (c - b)/a, a ≠ 0.
  • Like terms combine: 2x + 3x = 5x.
  • Distributive law: a(b + c) = ab + ac.

3) Geometry

  • Triangle angles sum to 180°.
  • Area of rectangle = lw; triangle = 1/2 bh; circle = πr².
  • Perimeter is the sum of side lengths.
