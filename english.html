English & Literature Notes

1) Grammar

  • Noun: a person, place, or thing. Verb: an action or state.
  • Subject-verb agreement: singular subject takes singular verb (The boy runs).
  • Tenses express time: past, present, future.

2) Comprehension

  • Identify main idea vs. supporting details.
  • Use context clues to infer word meanings.
  • Summarize passages briefly in your own words.

3) Literature

  • Plot: sequence of events. Setting: time and place.
  • Characterization shows personality through actions and dialogue.
  • Theme is the underlying message of a text.
